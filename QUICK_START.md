## 🔍 Using the Job Detector

### 5-Step Process

#### Step 1: Find Job Posting
Find a job posting you want to verify:
- LinkedIn
- Indeed
- Company website
- Job board
- Email

#### Step 2: Copy Text
Select and copy the entire job posting:
```
Ctrl+A to select all
Ctrl+C to copy
```

#### Step 3: Paste into App
1. Click in the text box (left side)
2. Paste the job text:
   ```
   Ctrl+V to paste
   ```
3. Or right-click → Paste

#### Step 4: Analyze
Click the blue **"Analyze with AI"** button

Wait 2-5 seconds for analysis...

#### Step 5: Review Results
On the right side, you'll see:
- **VERDICT** - Is it legit, suspicious, or a scam?
- **RISK SCORE** - 0-100 scale
- **RED FLAGS** - What's suspicious
- **GREEN FLAGS** - What's positive
- **ANALYSIS** - Detailed explanation

```
0-20%   🟢 Very Low Risk      - Safe to apply
20-40%  🟢 Low Risk           - Probably safe
40-60%  🟡 Medium Risk        - Be cautious
60-80%  🟡 High Risk          - Investigate
80-100% 🔴 Critical Risk      - Likely scam
```

## 💡 Red Flags to Watch For

The AI detects these warning signs:

❌ **Vague descriptions** - "Work from home, make $5000/month"
❌ **No company details** - No address, phone, or real info
❌ **Spelling errors** - Professional jobs rarely have typos
❌ **High pay, low skills** - "$3000/week, no experience needed"
❌ **Money upfront** - "Send $50 fee to get started"
❌ **Pressure tactics** - "Hire 5 people TODAY!"
❌ **Too good to be true** - Unrealistic benefits/pay
❌ **Grammar issues** - Broken English, awkward phrasing

## ⚠️ Important Tips

### Before You Apply
1. ✓ Use JobShield AI to check posting
2. ✓ Research company on Google
3. ✓ Call company to verify job posting
4. ✓ Check company website for careers page
5. ✓ Ask to speak with HR or hiring manager
6. ✓ Request video interview (legit companies do this)
7. ✓ Never pay any upfront fees

### Red Flags (Never Apply If)
✗ They ask for money upfront
✗ They ask for banking information
✗ They ask to wire transfer
✗ They want gift cards for "security"
✗ Contact only through email (no phone)
✗ They offer way more than similar jobs
✗ They pressure you to decide immediately

### Safe Practices
✓ Always verify company info independently
✓ Use official company email for contact
✓ Check LinkedIn for company and hiring manager
✓ Talk to current/former employees
✓ Never share SSN until offer letter
✓ Never download files from unknown sources

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "App won't open"
- Try clicking icon again
- Restart computer
- Check if Windows Defender blocked it (allow access)
- Reinstall the app

### "Analyze button doesn't work"
- Make sure job text is pasted
- Check internet connection
- Try shorter job posting first
- Wait a few seconds before clicking again

### "History not showing"
- Refresh the app
- Close and reopen
- Check if history was cleared
- History saves automatically

### "Slow analysis"
- Check internet speed (fast.com)
- Try shorter job posting
- Wait, API can be slow sometimes
- Retry the analysis

### "Getting error messages"
- Close app and restart
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache (in settings)
- Reinstall if problems persist
